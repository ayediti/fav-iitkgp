My Top 3 Locations in IIT Kharagpur!

The Clock Tower is one of the most beautiful sights in IIT Kharagpur. It looks especially majestic at night.

Main Building

The Main Building is not only beautiful but is also of national and academic importance. It does not fail to give its spectators goosebumps.

GymKhana Backside

This is the hotspot of IIT Kharagpur. Iykyk 😉! The Lakeside is beautiful and serene.
